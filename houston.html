Best events in Houston

It's always hot in Houston but they do enjoy the seasons - namely rodeo season, crawfish season and year-round J.J. Watt season. Head over to the Washington Ave. Arts District and pop in for open studios at Silver Street. Run the Memorial Park loop or two-step then grab fries at Frenchy's (you earned 'em). Wash down some patio (craft) beers with a kolache then hit one of the events below.
